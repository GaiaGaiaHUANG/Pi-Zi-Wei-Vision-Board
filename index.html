About Zi Wei

Zi Wei Dou Shu

紫微斗数 · Purple Star Astrology

Zi Wei Dou Shu is one of the most refined systems of Chinese astrology, practised for over a thousand years. Its name points to Zi Wei — the North Star — the still point around which everything else turns.

From the moment of your birth it draws a chart of 12 palaces, each a different room of a life: who you are, your wealth, your relationships, your travels, your home, your inner peace, and more. A constellation of stars falls across them, and the way those stars meet and answer one another sketches your tendencies, your seasons, and your possibilities.

It is not a verdict. It is a map — a way of seeing a whole life at once, and noticing how each part speaks to the others. Where you choose to walk on it remains entirely yours.

Twelve rooms, one life — none of them standing alone.

For example · Relationship

“I feel safe and seen, even on ordinary days.”
“We move through the world together, unhurried and at ease.”

Describe the feeling of having what you desire — rather than pointing to an object. Write as if it is already true.
